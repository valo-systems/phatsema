## Likely operational flows

> **Inference**

The public offering suggests the following flows may exist:

1. Procurement and receipt of chemicals, steel, HDPE, parts, and consumables.
2. Internal transfers from a central store to sites, vehicles, workshops, or projects.
3. Chemical blending, dispensing, or issue to dust-control installations.
4. Fabrication jobs consuming raw materials and producing made-to-order goods.
5. Rental-asset allocation, dispatch, inspection, servicing, meter capture, and return.
6. Client equipment intake, diagnosis, repair/rebuild, parts consumption, and handover.
7. Mobile plant mobilisation, site establishment, operation, and demobilisation.
8. Stock counts, write-offs, damaged stock, returns, and inter-site reconciliation.

These flows require confirmation through interviews and observation.
