# Company Profile

**Research date:** 18 July 2026  
**Evidence rule:** Website statements are public positioning and have not been independently verified.

## Phatsema Projects & Supplies

> **Published on website**

Phatsema Projects & Supplies says it was established in 2007, initially as a labour-hire company, and later developed into a multidisciplinary provider of plant hire, material handling, engineering, crushing and screening, steel fabrication, and related industrial services.

The business describes itself as:

- Headquartered in Brits, North West, South Africa.
- 100% black-owned.
- A Level 1 B-BBEE company.
- Active in mining, construction, civil works, engineering, and industrial services.
- Focused on cost-effective, environmentally responsible, and tailored solutions.

**Source:** [About Phatsema Projects & Supplies](https://phatsema.com/about-us/)  
**Accessed:** 18 July 2026

### Mission

> **Published on website**

To provide safe, reliable, quality projects and supplies that meet or exceed client expectations, while protecting the environment and empowering the communities in which the business operates.

**Source:** [Phatsema Projects & Supplies homepage](https://phatsema.com/)  
**Accessed:** 18 July 2026

### Vision

> **Published on website**

To become a leading and diverse projects-and-supply company in Africa's mining, construction, civil works, and engineering industries, while building long-term client relationships and empowering local communities.

**Source:** [Phatsema Projects & Supplies homepage](https://phatsema.com/)  
**Accessed:** 18 July 2026

### Values

> **Published on website**

- **Respect:** Prioritising customers and valuing different perspectives.

## Phatsema Mining

> **Published on website**

Phatsema Mining presents itself as a customer-focused provider of turnkey mining solutions. Its core public offering includes dust suppression, yellow machinery, projects and supplies, loading and haulage, and custom-manufactured mining hoses.

The business describes itself as:

- 100% black-owned.
- A Level 1 B-BBEE company.
- Focused on efficiency, sustainability, safety, quality, and cost effectiveness.
- A provider of customised solutions for mining operations.
- Active in South Africa and neighbouring African markets.

**Source:** [About Phatsema Mining](https://phatsemamining.co.za/about-us/)  
**Accessed:** 18 July 2026

### Mission

> **Published on website**

To create and grow sustainable value for stakeholders by applying best practices.

**Source:** [Phatsema Mining homepage](https://phatsemamining.co.za/)  
**Accessed:** 18 July 2026

### Vision

> **Published on website**

To be recognised as a world-class turnkey-solution organisation.

**Source:** [Phatsema Mining homepage](https://phatsemamining.co.za/)  
**Accessed:** 18 July 2026

### Philosophy and values

The Mining website emphasises fast turnaround, long-term customer relationships, and going beyond the minimum expected service. It lists quality, innovation and sustainability, customer relationships, and safe and efficient operations as core values.

**Source:** [Phatsema Mining homepage](https://phatsemamining.co.za/); [About Phatsema Mining](https://phatsemamining.co.za/about-us/)

## Relationship between the businesses

> **Requires confirmation**

The websites show a close relationship: they cross-link, share an address, name the same CEO, and publish overlapping services. However, the sites do not establish whether Phatsema Mining and Phatsema Projects & Supplies are:

- Separate registered companies.
- Divisions of one company.
- Trading names.
- Brands within a wider group.

The back-office data model, access controls, financial reporting, and invoicing should not be designed until this relationship is confirmed.

## Known, inferred, and unknown

### Known from the websites

- The Phatsema name covers mining, supply, engineering, logistics, plant, fabrication, and dust-control activities.
- The public headquarters is in Brits.
- Both sites claim 100% black ownership and Level 1 B-BBEE status.
- Both sites name Tshepiso E. Mkhonto as CEO.

### Inferred

- The businesses may share leadership, premises, marketing, and some operational resources.
- A future back office may need entity, branch, site, warehouse, and cost-centre dimensions.

### Unknown

- Employee count, active customer count, and completed-project count.
- Exact operating sites and warehouse locations.

- Whether the advertised cross-border footprint includes permanent facilities, projects, agents, or clients.
