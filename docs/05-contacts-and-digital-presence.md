## Website inconsistencies and broken contact links

> **Published on website**

The following visible links appear incorrectly configured:

1. Several Projects & Supplies “Get in contact” buttons use `mailto:infophatsema.co.za`, which is missing the `@` symbol.
2. Several service pages use `info@phatsema-mining.co.za`, although the page belongs to the `phatsema.com` website. This may be intentional cross-entity routing but requires confirmation.
3. The Projects & Supplies footer displays `lebo@phatsema.co.za`, while its underlying link was observed as `mailto:ebo@phatsema.co.za`.
4. Capitalisation varies for the Mining departmental email addresses. Email domains are generally case-insensitive, but standardised lowercase addresses should be confirmed.
5. The customer-reference links on the Mining website point to a JDC Digital brochure rather than a Phatsema reference document.

**Sources:** [Projects & Supplies homepage](https://phatsema.com/); [Projects & Supplies service pages](https://phatsema.com/projects-supplies-2/); [Customer References](https://phatsemamining.co.za/customer-references/)  
**Accessed:** 18 July 2026

> **Requires confirmation**

- Whether the published phone numbers and mailboxes are current.
- Whether service-page enquiries should route to Projects & Supplies or Mining.
- The correct email address for Lebo.
- Which contact information should appear on contracts, invoices, purchase orders, and system notifications.
- Whether any social accounts are shared group accounts rather than entity-specific accounts.
